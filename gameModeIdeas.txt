	Single Player Versus

Game Varaiants
	Mitosis
	Balls grow from small to large and then split.

	Speed Gradient
	Balls go from green to red over time and grow faster. After the balls turn bright red they split into two green balls.

	Powers of 2
	Balls split into two after each paddle collision.

 	Gravity
 	Two player gravity applies to the ball, bottom is really bouncy.
